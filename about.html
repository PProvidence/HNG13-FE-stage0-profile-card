I've always been drawn to the intersection of design and technology, where creativity meets problem-solving.

Areas Where I'm Building Confidence

While I'm confident in many areas of frontend development, I recognize that growth requires honesty about where I can improve. Here are the areas where I'm actively working to strengthen my skills:

  • Backend Development: While I have foundational knowledge, I'm still building confidence in areas like API design, authentication systems, and database optimization.
  • Advanced State Management: Complex state management patterns in large-scale applications, particularly with tools like Redux and Zustand, are areas I'm continuously learning.
  • Testing Practices: While I understand the importance of unit and integration testing, I'm working to make testing a more natural part of my workflow and learn advanced testing strategies.
  • System Design: Thinking through scalable architecture and making strategic technical decisions for large applications is something I'm actively developing.
  • DevOps & CI/CD: While I can deploy applications, I want to deepen my understanding of containerization, deployment pipelines, and infrastructure management.
